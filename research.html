Research Interests

My research interests lie in applications of analytic modeling to computer architecture performance, power, and energy projections. Using integrated performance, power, energy, and area models, I contributed to our dark silicon projections for future CPU and GPU systems, an interactive version of which is available at the Vertical Website.
